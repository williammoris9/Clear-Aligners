What Are Clear Aligners?

Clear Aligners are transparent, custom-made trays designed to gradually move your teeth into proper alignment. They are made of smooth, BPA-free plastic and fit snugly over your teeth. Unlike braces, there are no metal brackets or wires—just a discreet, comfortable solution that most people won’t even notice. Every few weeks, you’ll switch to a new set of aligners that gently guide your teeth into place.

Benefits of Choosing Clear Aligners

  • Nearly Invisible: The biggest advantage of Clear Aligners is their discreet appearance. You can smile confidently without worrying about metal braces showing.
  • Comfortable Fit: The aligners are custom-crafted for your teeth, ensuring a smooth, irritation-free experience.
  • Removable: You can remove them while eating, brushing, or flossing, making oral hygiene simple and meals more enjoyable.
  • Predictable Results: Using 3D imaging technology, orthodontists can show you the projected outcome of your treatment before it even begins.
  • Fewer Appointments: Since there are no wires to adjust, you’ll need fewer visits to the orthodontist—saving time while maintaining progress.

How Do Clear Aligners Work?

The process begins with a detailed consultation at Sabinsky Orthodontics, DDS. During your visit, we use digital scanning to capture precise images of your teeth. This allows our team to create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs. You’ll receive a series of Clear Aligners, each slightly adjusted to continue moving your teeth step-by-step toward their ideal position. Over several months, you’ll start noticing visible improvements in your smile.

Are Clear Aligners Right for You?

Clear Aligners are suitable for most teens and adults looking to correct mild to moderate misalignment issues, such as crowding, gaps, or bite problems. If you’ve been hesitant about braces due to appearance or lifestyle concerns, this treatment offers a discreet and flexible alternative.
